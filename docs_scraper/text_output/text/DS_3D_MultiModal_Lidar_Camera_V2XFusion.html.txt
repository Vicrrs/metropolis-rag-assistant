formDeepStream-3...DeepStream-3D Multi-Modal V2XFusion Setup#The pipeline efficiently processes data from 4 cameras and 4 corresponding LiDARs, inferencing the 3D bboxes with a new model which combines theBEVFusionmodel and theBEVHeightmodel together to get more accurate detection result. This model is pre-trained by theDAIR-V2X dataset. The pipeline seamlessly employsds3d::datatfilerfor triton inference. Ultimately, users can inferencing 4 cameras’ videos/images and the corresponding LiDARs’ data to detect 3D bboxes of different objects in road.
